What is Black River Systems, Inc.?

Black River Systems is the business vehicle of Mike Mohrbacher, Principal Consultant, a software developer with decades of professional experience. Black River Systems has been incorporated in the State of Illinois (File#: 5890-4201) since 1996.

What services do you offer?

We offer custom software development and consulting services to customers in the financial, product development, and insurance industries.

We specialize in delivering solutions using Microsoft server technologies and standards-based web technologies.

Any special areas of expertise?

In the financial sector, we delivered Bloomberg’s high-speed B-Pipe interface to the Options Clearing Corporation, the clearing house for all options trades in the United States. The interface accepts and delivers timely quote data to OCC’s mark-to-market mechanisms.

In the insurance field, we designed, implemented, and delivered a rule-based insurance quote engine for Swiss Re, a leading re-insurance company in Switzerland.

In product development, we designed and implemented major software components for companies in the industrial and commercial sector. For Bio-Imaging Research (now part of Varian Medical Systems), we designed and developed a control suite for industrial, research, and medical x-ray scanners. For Progeny Dental, we developed a control suite and user-interface components for digital capture and management of dental x-rays. For ArialPhone, we designed and developed a control suite for a pioneering voice-controlled, user-wearable phone system.

Where will you work?

We offer our services on-site in the Chicago metropolitan area. Off-site development is available at our world headquarters.

Why should I hire you over somebody else?

We like what we are doing. Software is our craft. We have been doing it for a long time and we have gotten really, really good at it.

Besides our principal consultant, we have access to a close-knit group of very talented software developers. This group has assembled and successfully collaborated on a number of projects.
